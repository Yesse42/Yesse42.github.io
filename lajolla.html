The Climate of La Jolla

There is a weather station located on UCSD's campus at SIO's Southwest Fisheries building. Although its period of record is a short 8 years, it can still grant some valuable insight into the climate on campus. Let's take a look at some summary statistics I generated for it!

As should be apparent, UCSD's climate takes moderation to the absolute extreme. With the change in average high temperature between the coldest and warmest months standing at a mere 11ºF, and the difference in average low temperatures being 13ºF, the seasons here are pretty muted, to say the least
